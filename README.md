## 🌟 Overview

Salary Toolkit is a lightweight, privacy-first web application designed to help Indian professionals better understand their salary.

Instead of being "just another calculator", the project focuses on financial awareness by providing actionable insights based on salary inputs.

All calculations happen **locally in your browser**. No salary information is stored or transmitted.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🌙 Night Shift Allowance Calculator

Supports two calculation methods:

- Estimated Company Formula
- Fixed Allowance Per Night

Advanced customization:

- Working Days Per Year
- Night Allowance Percentage

Outputs:

- Per Night Allowance
- Total Allowance
- Estimated Yearly Value
- Personalized financial suggestions

---

### 📈 Appraisal Hike Calculator

Calculate:

- Hike %
- Monthly Salary Increase
- Yearly Salary Increase

Provides motivational career guidance based on the hike percentage.

---

### 💰 Take Home Salary Calculator

Estimate:

- Monthly Take Home
- Yearly Take Home

Based on annual salary and tax percentage.

---
